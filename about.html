GREETING

Welcome to MAST

Welcome to MAST — Management and Strategy Thinkers at Indiana University.

MAST was founded on the belief that the most impactful leaders are those who combine analytical rigor with a broad, humanistic perspective. In today's rapidly evolving business landscape, strategic thinking is not merely an advantage — it is essential.

Our community brings together ambitious students from diverse academic backgrounds, united by a shared passion for strategy, business, and meaningful impact. Through workshops, case competitions, and industry partnerships, we strive to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world application.

We invite you to be part of a community that challenges assumptions, celebrates intellectual curiosity, and cultivates the next generation of strategic thinkers.

— MAST Leadership
