About MAST

Empowering Strategic Thinkers of Tomorrow

Daniel Ryu Founder

FOUNDER'S MESSAGE

Welcome to MAST

The world doesn't stand still — and neither should we. What separates those who lead from those who follow is not just knowledge, but the ability to think clearly, adapt boldly, and grow together.

MAST was built on that belief. We choose discomfort over complacency — looking beyond our own fields and asking harder questions.

We believe in multidisciplinary thinking. MAST exists to expand the toolbox — connecting dots across industries and disciplines that others might never think to combine.

And above all, we believe in people. Through our network of Korean professionals and alumni worldwide, we build connections that last well beyond graduation.

— Daniel Ryu, Founder

OUR PURPOSE

Mission & Vision

◈

Mission

To cultivate leaders who read change before it happens — equipping members with the critical thinking, strategic perspective, and global network to lead in any industry.

◈

Vision

A community where choosing discomfort is the norm — where multidisciplinary thinking and genuine human connection drive lasting impact far beyond the classroom.

◈

Network

To build a sustainable cycle of exchange between students and Korean professionals worldwide — where every connection made today becomes a resource for the next generation.

History

MAST Founded

MAST was established at Indiana University with a founding cohort of strategic thinkers committed to bridging business theory and practice.

Alumni Career Workshop

Invited MAST alumni working at top consulting and business firms to share firsthand career insights with current members through an interactive employment workshop.

Second Alumni Career Workshop

Hosted a second alumni career workshop, expanding the program with one-on-one mentorship sessions, mock interviews, and recruiting strategy discussions with professionals across multiple industries.
